About Me

Mechanical engineer with over 12 years of experience in industrial design.

Core experience in pressure vessels, steam boilers, piping systems, steel structures, and related industrial equipment for the energy, chemical, oil & gas, and food industries.

Holds a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Mechanical Engineering and Mechatronics (2006–2012). From 2014 to 2016, worked on end-to-end mechanical development of high-end audio equipment for a startup in Warsaw, including full technical documentation, certification, and setup of production and supply chains. Over the following nine years, worked in Germany as a design engineer, project engineer, and project manager on pressure vessels, steam boilers, chemical reactors, steel structures and process piping in accordance with DIN EN, AD 2000, and ASME standards, contributing to international projects for clients such as Linde, Accsys, BAT, Nestlé, Shell, ADM, Hitachi, and others. Additionally, as a side project, I co-founded Dotyk Fingers, a startup focused on developing body-powered finger prostheses for people with disabilities, where I served as CEO and led the product's technical development.
